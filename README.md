## Run Locally

**Prerequisites:**  Node.js


1. Install dependencies:
   `npm install`
2. Set the `GEMINI_API_KEY` in [.env.local](.env.local) to your Gemini API key
3. Run the app:
   `npm run dev`

# CarbonPulse User Journey

## From Awareness to Climate Impact

CarbonPulse is designed as a complete sustainability journey that guides users from discovering their environmental footprint to becoming active contributors to global climate solutions.

---

# Stage 1: Discover

### "How sustainable am I?"

The journey begins when a user signs up using Google, Apple, Microsoft, or email authentication.

Upon onboarding, CarbonPulse introduces users to their sustainability profile through an engaging assessment covering:

* Transportation habits
* Home energy usage
*
* Dietary preferences
* Shopping behavior
* Travel frequency
* Waste generation
* Digital consumption

Users may also connect external services such as:

* Bank accounts
* Credit cards
* Utility providers
* Ride-sharing platforms
* Smart devices
* Mobility applications

Within minutes, CarbonPulse generates the user's first Carbon Score and Sustainability Grade.

**Outcome:**
Users gain their first clear understanding of their environmental impact.

---

# Stage 2: Understand

### "Where do my emissions come from?"

The AI Sustainability Coach analyzes user behavior and breaks emissions into understandable categories.

Interactive dashboards reveal:

* Transportation footprint
* Food emissions
* Energy consumption
* Shopping impact
* Travel footprint
* Waste contribution

AI-generated insights explain:

* Major emission sources
* Behavioral patterns
* Hidden carbon costs
* Benchmark comparisons

Users can compare themselves against:

* City averages
* National averages
* Global averages
* Net-zero targets

**Outcome:**
Awareness becomes understanding.

---

# Stage 3: Act

### "What should I do next?"

Instead of generic advice, CarbonPulse creates personalized sustainability action plans.

Examples include:

* Replace two weekly car trips with public transportation
* Reduce home electricity consumption by 10%
* Switch to plant-based meals twice weekly
* Purchase fewer high-emission products
* Join local environmental initiatives

Each recommendation includes:

* Potential CO₂ reduction
* Estimated cost
* Difficulty level
* Impact score
* Time commitment

The AI prioritizes actions with the highest return on environmental impact.

**Outcome:**
Users know exactly how to reduce their footprint.

---

# Stage 4: Track

### "Am I making progress?"

CarbonPulse automatically tracks sustainability actions through connected services and user activity.

Examples:

* Bike commute detected
* Electricity reduction recorded
* Flight emissions added
* Sustainable purchases recognized
* Water savings measured

Users monitor progress through:

* Daily carbon reports
* Weekly sustainability summaries
* Monthly impact reviews
* Real-time Carbon Score updates

The platform continuously learns and improves recommendations.

**Outcome:**
Users see measurable improvements in real time.

---

# Stage 5: Engage

### "Can I do this with others?"

Behavior change accelerates when it becomes social.

CarbonPulse introduces:

* Sustainability communities
* Local groups
* Friends and family networks
* Workplace teams
* University cohorts

Users participate in challenges such as:

* No Car Week
* Plastic-Free Month
* Plant-Based Challenge
* Energy Saver Challenge

Community leaderboards foster healthy competition while encouraging collective climate action.

**Outcome:**
Sustainability becomes a shared experience.

---

# Stage 6: Achieve

### "My actions are creating impact."

As users complete activities, they earn:

* Green Points
* Achievement Badges
* XP Rewards
* Sustainability Streaks
* Community Recognition

Users progress through levels:

* Eco Starter
* Green Explorer
* Climate Champion
* Carbon Warrior
* Net Zero Hero

Milestones celebrate meaningful achievements and reinforce positive habits.

**Outcome:**
Motivation remains high through visible progress and rewards.

---

# Stage 7: Offset

### "How do I address unavoidable emissions?"

For emissions that cannot yet be eliminated, CarbonPulse provides access to verified climate projects.

Users can support:

* Tree planting
* Mangrove restoration
* Renewable energy projects
* Ocean cleanup initiatives
* Biodiversity conservation programs

Every contribution includes:

* Verified impact metrics
* Project transparency
* Carbon certificates
* Ongoing progress updates

**Outcome:**
Users achieve a balanced and responsible carbon footprint.

---

# Stage 8: Lead

### "How can I inspire others?"

Advanced users become climate leaders within the CarbonPulse ecosystem.

They can:

* Create sustainability groups
* Launch local initiatives
* Mentor new members
* Organize community events
* Advocate for environmental change

Organizations gain access to ESG dashboards that measure collective impact across teams and departments.

**Outcome:**
Individual action scales into community transformation.

---

# Stage 9: Transform

### "What does long-term impact look like?"

Using AI forecasting, CarbonPulse projects sustainability outcomes over:

* 3 months
* 6 months
* 12 months
* 5 years

Users visualize how today's decisions influence future emissions and climate impact.

The platform demonstrates:

* Total emissions avoided
* Trees-equivalent impact
* Water saved
* Energy conserved
* Community contributions

**Outcome:**
Users see tangible evidence that their actions contribute to a more sustainable future.

---

# The CarbonPulse Flywheel

Measure → Understand → Act → Track → Engage → Achieve → Offset → Lead → Transform

Each cycle improves awareness, strengthens habits, increases environmental impact, and motivates deeper participation.

CarbonPulse transforms sustainability from a one-time calculation into a lifelong journey of measurable climate action.
